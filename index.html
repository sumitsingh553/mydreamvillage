Social Welfare Projects

Our projects are aimed at enhancing the welfare of the entire village. Our aim is to make a club of all the citizens of the village (Hathwala, Jind), who have some progressive outlook and are interested in the betterment of the village as a whole. It will purely be a non-political group and its aim will be to uplift the standard of education in the village, to take social welfare measures for the betterment of society as a whole among others.
